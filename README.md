# Multi-Channel Digital Marketing Performance Dashboard — Looker Studio

## 📌 Business Problem & Objective
A global e-commerce brand was running paid campaigns across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and TikTok Ads without a centralized source of truth. The marketing team was struggling to calculate total Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) and identify which channels were causing budget leakage due to low conversion rates.

The objective of this project was to design a centralized, interactive dashboard in Looker Studio that unifies cross-channel metrics, tracks the customer acquisition funnel, and optimizes budget allocation.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Tracked
* **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** Monitored across channels to ensure profitability benchmarks (> 3.5x) are met.
* **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition):** Tracked dynamically against historical baselines to flag rising ad costs.
* **Funnel Conversion Rate (CR):** From Click-Through Rate (CTR) to Add-to-Cart and final Purchase.

---

## 📈 Business Key Results & Actionable Insights
* **Budget Optimization:** Discovered that Meta Ads had a **22% lower CPA** compared to Google Ads for the same target audience, leading to a recommended 15% budget reallocation.
